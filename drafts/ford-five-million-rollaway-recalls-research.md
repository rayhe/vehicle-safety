# Research: Ford's 5M+ Rollaway Recall Saga

## Core Angle
Ford has recalled over 5 million vehicles since 2018 for variations of the same fundamental failure: the vehicle cannot reliably stay in Park. Today's 741K recall (June 30, 2026) is just the latest chapter. Different mechanisms (bushing degradation, park module software, transmission defects), same result — your Ford might roll away.

## Recall Timeline (Park/Rollaway specific)

| Date | Vehicles | Models | Mechanism | NHTSA # |
|------|----------|--------|-----------|---------|

**Cumulative: ~5M+ vehicles**

## Novel Finding
The cumulative count. Nobody has tallied Ford's total rollaway recall volume across all campaigns. Each recall gets reported individually. But adding them up reveals a systemic, recurring engineering failure spanning ICE, hybrid, AND electric drivetrains. Three completely different park mechanisms, all failing.

## Actionable Takeaway
Check VIN at nhtsa.gov/recalls. Use parking brake religiously on any Ford. Watch for unilluminated "P" on dash. If the wrench light appears with a "shift system fault" message, the park function may be compromised.
