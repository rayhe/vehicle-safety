**Math verification:**

**Verifiability:** All key claims trace to cited sources. The exposure-window math is transparent with stated inputs and assumptions.

**Verdict:** PASS. Strongest section of the article. The exposure-window metric is a genuine original contribution with honest methodology disclosure.
