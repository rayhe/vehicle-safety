# Research: BMW Starter Fire Third Recall (2026)

## Breaking News — July 23, 2026
BMW recalls 318,495 U.S. vehicles for starter motor overheating/fire risk. NHTSA announced today.

## The 2026 BMW Starter Fire Recall Timeline
1. **Feb 2026:** ~87,394 US vehicles (3 Series, 4 Series, 5 Series, 6 Series GT, X3, X4, Z4 + Toyota Supra). 575K worldwide. First identified Sep 2025 after 3 vehicles caught fire. Valeo supplier.
2. **Jul 14, 2026:** 29,119 US PHEVs (330e iPerformance, 530e/xDrive, 740Le xDrive). Expansion to plug-in hybrids. Same starter relay corrosion.
3. **Jul 23, 2026 (TODAY):** 318,495 US vehicles. Massive expansion covering: 2 Series (G42), 3 Series (G20/21), 4 Series (G22/23/26), 5 Series (G30/31), 6 Series (G32), 7 Series (G11/12), X3 (G01), X4 (G02), X5 (G05), X6 (G06), Z4 (G29).

**Combined 2026 total: ~434,000+ US vehicles recalled for the same root cause.**

## Root Cause
- Valeo-supplied starter motors
- Metallic material buildup in electrical relay chamber from excessive abrasion
- Water ingress causes corrosion → short circuit → overheating → fire
- Can ignite while driving OR while parked with ignition off
