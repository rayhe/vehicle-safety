# Research: denso-fifty-cent-part-six-million-toyotas

## News Hook
CarBuzz (Jul 4, 2026): "How A 50-Cent Denso Part Triggered Toyota's 3.3 Million-Vehicle Recall Nightmare"
- Denso fuel pump impeller defect: insufficient resin density → absorbs fuel → deforms → engine stalls
- Original recall Jan 2020: 3.34M vehicles
- Expanded Jan 2025 and Apr 2026
- Total: 5.8M+ vehicles globally (Toyota + Lexus)
- Other brands affected: Honda, Acura, Subaru, Mazda
- Class-action settled Dec 2022: $212-288M + $28.5M legal fees
- 15-year warranty extension on fuel pumps
