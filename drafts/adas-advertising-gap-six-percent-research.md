# Research Notes: ADAS Advertising Gap

## News Hook
- **Reuters/Brembo/Economist Enterprise survey** (published Jul 14, 2026)
  - 1,000+ transport specialists across 10 countries (US, France, Germany, Italy, UK, China, India, Brazil, Japan, South Korea)
  - 30% cite ADAS misuse/misunderstanding as #1 safety risk
  - 24% (separate question) point to "increasingly distracting in-vehicle features"
  - **2/3 say advertising overstates ADAS capabilities** — "creating unrealistic expectations"
  - Ignacio Alvarez, Brembo R&D Chief: systems need clear visibility of "what they can do and what they cannot do"
  - Pratima Singh, Economist Enterprise: "The real risk is the interface between humans and machines"
  - Context: China tightening ADAS oversight after fatal Xiaomi crash; Europe weighing FSD rollout
  - Source: Reuters

## EU Peer-Reviewed Study
- **Journal of Safety Research** (published Jan 2026, PubMed PMID 41338785)
- "Safety impact of advanced driver assistance systems in Europe in 2030"
- VTT Technical Research Centre of Finland
- Findings:
  - ALL mandatory ADAS combined = **6% injury crash reduction** by 2030 vs 2019 baseline
  - AEB alone = **5%** of that 6%
  - Advisory ISA = **1%**
  - LKA = **0.2%**
  - Reverse cameras = **0.2%**
  - Conclusion: ADAS should be "combined with other measures" — alone insufficient for EU 50% death reduction target

## FARS Data
- Total deaths in dataset: 187,058
- Pre-2018 MY deaths: 172,849 (92.4%)
- Post-2018 MY deaths: 14,209 (7.6%)
- Post-2020 MY deaths: 5,955 (3.2%)
- Top post-2018 MY killers: Camry (490), F-150 (452), Corolla (448), Civic (417), Sentra (394)

## Key Insight
- Industry experts say ADAS misuse is #1 risk
- That risk applies to 7.6% of the body count (post-2018 MY vehicles)
- Within that 7.6%, the EU study says AEB does 83% of the ADAS safety work
- AEB is FREE and standard on all new cars since 2023 (voluntary industry commitment)
- The features marketed as premium (FSD $15K, BlueCruise $2.1K, Super Cruise $2.5K) correspond to the 17% of ADAS benefit (LKA + ISA + cameras)
- Consumer takeaway: AEB is already saving your life, you already have it, nobody markets it

## Differentiation from Existing Stories
- gm-adas-86-percent-illusion: covers one company's self-serving study; this uses independent expert survey + EU peer review
- nhtsa-adas-tests-wrong-fleet: covers NHTSA testing wrong vehicles; this is about marketing/consumer perception
- speed-ads-safety-silence: about speed marketing; this is about ADAS marketing specifically
