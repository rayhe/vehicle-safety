# Research: Ford Recalled 20 Million Vehicles Last Year. It Sold 2.2 Million.

## Angle
Ford recalled nearly 20 million vehicles in the 12 months ending March 2026 — roughly 9x its annual US sales of 2.2 million. The Lincoln Aviator is projected to accumulate 92 lifetime recalls (industry median: 3.9). Ford is not just a car company with a recall problem. It is a recall company that occasionally sells cars.

## Primary Sources (3+)

### 1. iSeeCars / NHTSA Analysis (via Detroit News / TheAutoExec)
- Ford recalled ~20M vehicles in 12 months to March 2026
- More than every other major automaker **combined**
- Toyota #2 at ~4M, Stellantis #3 at 3.4M
- iSeeCars projects Lincoln Aviator: 92 lifetime recalls (industry median 3.9)
- Lincoln Corsair, Ford Maverick, Ford Bronco fill 4 of top 5 lifetime-recall spots
- Lowest-recall brands: Subaru, Honda, Toyota, Mazda
- Ford attributes bulk to 2015-2022 model-year vehicles (pre-Farley)
- Analyst Karl Brauer: Maverick launched 2021, fully within Farley's tenure
- Source: theautoexec.com, cbtnews.com

### 2. Ford Motor Company — 2025 US Sales
- 2025 US sales: 2,204,124 vehicles (up 6% YoY)
- Market share: 13.2%
- F-Series: 828,832 units (record)
- Hybrid sales: 228,072 (record, up 21.7%)
- Source: Ford press release via barchart.com, tradingview.com

### 3. NHTSA — Ford Recall Record & Fine
- 152 recalls issued in 2025 (shattered previous record of 77 by GM in 2014)
- 34 recalls in 2026 already by mid-year (Autoblog: "nearly tripling last year's total")
- $165 million fine (Nov 2024) for dragging feet on rearview camera recalls
- 27% of 2025 recalls were re-recalls (software updates that reported failed updates as successful)
- Source: NHTSA, autoblog.com, hotcars.com

### 4. FARS Data (vehicle-safety.org / NHTSA FARS 2014-2023)
- F-150: 9,194 deaths, rate 1.04/100M VMT, 20,066 fatal crashes
- Ford Mustang: 2,739 deaths, rate 6.02 (nearly 6x fleet average)
- Ford Ranger: 3,089 deaths, rate 2.91
- Ford Explorer: 3,797 deaths, rate 1.54
- Ford Escape: 2,284 deaths, rate 0.95
- Ford Focus: 3,046 deaths, rate 2.52
- Ford Fusion: 2,168 deaths, rate 1.23
- Ford total across FARS models: ~35,000 deaths (2014-2023)

## Key Numbers
- **20 million**: vehicles recalled in 12 months
- **2.2 million**: vehicles sold in 2025
- **~9:1**: recall-to-sales ratio
- **92**: projected lifetime recalls for Lincoln Aviator
- **3.9**: industry median lifetime recalls per model
- **$165 million**: NHTSA fine for delayed recalls
- **27%**: share of 2025 recalls that were re-recalls
- **152**: individual recall campaigns in 2025

## Novel Contribution
Cross-referencing Ford's recall volume with its sales volume to produce the 9:1 ratio. Nobody has framed it this way. Also: the Lincoln Aviator's projected 92-recall lifetime means roughly one recall every 4 months of ownership for 30 years. At what point does a vehicle become not a product but a recall subscription service?
