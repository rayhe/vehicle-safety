### ⚠️ Counting Error — "twenty-two other states"

The sentence reads: *"larger than Connecticut, Utah, Nevada, Iowa, and twenty-two other states below them on the census rolls."*

This implies 4 named + 22 others = **26 states smaller**. Actual count: **22 states** total are smaller than 4,097,000. The word "other" is the problem — it should be **"eighteen other states"** (22 − 4 = 18), or restructure.

**Suggested fix:** *"larger than twenty-two states, from Connecticut down to Wyoming"* or *"larger than Connecticut, Utah, Nevada, Iowa, and eighteen other states below them."*

**Trivial wording fix on otherwise correct data.** Must be fixed before publish.
