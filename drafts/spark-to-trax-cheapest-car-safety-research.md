# Research: GM's Cheapest Car — From 75% Lethality to 83% Safer

## Angle
GM discontinued the Chevrolet Spark (2022) and Sonic (2020) — two subcompact sedans that were among the cheapest new cars in America. Their replacement, the Chevrolet Trax crossover, now occupies the "cheapest Chevy" slot at $21,700. The Trax's FARS death rate is 83% lower than the Spark's. This isn't about drunk driving or reckless behavior — the Spark had the LOWEST impairment rate of any GM small car. The vehicle itself was the problem.

## FARS Data (Source 1)

### Chevrolet Spark
- Deaths: 517
- Fleet: 350,000
- Rate: 1.28 per 100M VMT
- Crashes: 695
- Lethality: 74.4% (3 in 4 crashes kill someone)
- Toxicology: 16.2% any impairment (LOWEST of all GM small cars)
- Production: 1995-2022 (US sales 2013-2022 as 3rd gen)
- MSRP: ~$13,600 (2022, cheapest new car in America)

### Chevrolet Sonic
- Deaths: 494
- Fleet: 306,250
- Rate: 1.40 per 100M VMT
- Crashes: 655
- Lethality: 75.4%
- Toxicology: 20.0% any impairment
- Production: 2004-2020
- MSRP: ~$15,420 (2020)

### Chevrolet Trax (replacement)
- Deaths: 191
- Fleet: 700,000
- Rate: 0.22 per 100M VMT
- Crashes: 355
- Lethality: 53.8%
- Toxicology: 21.5% any impairment
- Production: 2013-present (2nd gen 2024+)
- MSRP: $21,700 (2026)
- Sales: 200,000+ in 2025 (Jalopnik)

### Comparison
- Trax fleet is 2× Spark fleet, yet has 63% fewer deaths
- Trax death rate 0.22 vs Spark 1.28 = 83% reduction
- Trax death rate 0.22 vs Sonic 1.40 = 84% reduction
- Trax lethality 53.8% vs Spark 74.4% = 28% reduction
- Spark impairment (16.2%) is LOWER than Trax (21.5%) — sober drivers were dying in the Spark

## Story Thesis
The Spark was marketed as affordable transportation. It was the cheapest new car in America. It was also a 74% death sentence in any crash — and its drivers were the most sober of any GM small car, so impairment wasn't the explanation. When GM killed the Spark and Sonic and moved entry-level buyers into the Trax crossover, the death rate dropped 83%. The extra $8,000 and 740 extra pounds buy something no option package can: surviving a crash.
