## Key Data Points

### Brand-Level
- 14,456 total deaths across 19 models
- 13 of 19 (68%) above class median death rate
- #5 brand by absolute deaths but #1 in deaths-per-fleet-size among top-10 brands
- More deaths than Hyundai (6,854) + BMW (2,196) + VW (2,234) combined

### Sedan Catastrophe
- Maxima: 5.11 rate (class median 0.68) — 7.5x the median. #1 deadliest sedan.
- Altima: 2.88 rate — 4.2x median. 4,787 deaths.
- Sentra: 2.13 rate — 3.1x median. 2,571 deaths.
- Three sedans combined: 8,902 deaths.

### SUVs Above Median
- Xterra: 1.39 (4.0x SUV median of 0.35)
- Pathfinder: 0.93 (2.7x)
- Kicks: 0.69 (2.0x)
- Armada: 0.55 (1.6x)
- Murano: 0.43 (1.2x)
- Juke: 0.43 (1.2x)
- Only Rogue (0.35, exactly at median) escapes

### The Rogue Exception
- Rogue: 0.35 rate, at median. Their best-seller. 2,188K fleet.
- Proves Nissan CAN build a safe car when the commercial stakes demand it.
